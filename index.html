Stayed 18 Nov, 2019

It was very nice hotel with cleanliness. Staff behavior was good and polite. They welcome us very well. Issue was only that Lift was not in working and we were allotted to 3rd floor and amenities articles were in corner of gallery which were giving bad feeling. Breakfast was good and support of the staff was also very nice. Location is not good as per atmosphere, it is very nearby most of the popular places but self location in a narrow street is not good. Overall it was a good experience and could recommend.

Stayed 21 Sep, 2018

Everything was good and as per Novotel standards. The staff was professional, courteous and extremely helpful. Ms BB at the reception was always cheerful and kind enough to help us with all our requirements. The bell boy who doubled as the driver was also helpful. We are Also thankful to team for allowing us to park our car at their hotel even after check out since we had plans to stay at Phi Phi Island for a couple of days. All in all all comfortable stay. These are really difficult times for everyone and we really admire and appreciate the hospitality of the whole team.
